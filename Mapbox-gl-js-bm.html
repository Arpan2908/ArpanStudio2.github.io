This is a map made for the American Red Cross. It is a humanitarian organization providing disaster relief, and emergency assistance and preparedness education in the United States. You can learn more about the organization at www.redcross.org .A guide for the colors they use could be found at https://www.redcross.org/content/dam/redcross/atg/PDFs/BrandPoster.pdf. A shade of grey is used for water to show contrast between the land and water and to resemble primary colors used by the organization.
